## Verdict — Best-Fit ICP for César's Current Profile

**Primary: C (Founder BR) — 8.6/10.** César's exact stack (Next + Supabase + TS + Tailwind) is the default BR indie/MVP toolkit, mid-level + full-stack breadth is what founders pay for, PT-native removes friction, and UTC-3 makes him reachable. The only weak axis (track record depth) matters less here because founders judge on "can you ship this MVP in 6 weeks?" not on portfolio polish.

**Strong secondary: D (Agência BR) — 8.4/10** and **A (Recruiter BR) — 8.4/10.** D leverages the PHP/WP half of the stack that A and C ignore; A is the safety-net path with most volume.

**Defer: B (International Remote) — 5.4/10.** Three compounding gaps (mid seniority, EN polish, portfolio depth). Aspirational target post-portfolio v2 + 2 more shipped products + EN copy work.

**Recommendation:** optimize portfolio v2 primarily for ICP C, secondarily for D, with A as fallback. Do not over-invest in B until shipped-evidence and EN copy gaps close.
